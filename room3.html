Twin Room

A twin room is a hotel room with two single beds that can accommodate two people. Twin rooms are suitable for friends or colleagues traveling together who prefer to have separate beds. Twin rooms are usually priced similarly to double rooms.The twin room and double room seem to be the same. Both types of these hotel rooms are designed for two people. The twin room has two single beds. The double room has one double bed, so-called, a marital bed.
